ABOUT THE TOURNAMENT

Celebrating the spirit of KNBHS alumni through football, camaraderie, and friendly competition.

Our Mission

The KNBHS Alumni Football Tournament was founded to reconnect former students, celebrate their achievements, and foster networking through sportsmanship.

Over the years, the tournament has grown to include multiple batches, creating a vibrant community and providing a platform for healthy competition and lifelong friendships.
